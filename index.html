Learn More About Oral Health

What are Cavities?

Cavities are damaged areas in your teeth that turn into small holes over time. They happen when bacteria feed on sugars and create acid that wears away your enamel.

How do we get them?

  • Sugary or sticky foods
  • Missing brush/floss sessions
  • Plaque buildup

What is Gingivitis?

The early stage of gum disease. It's reversible! It happens when plaque irritates your gumline, making gums red or swollen.

Signs to watch for:

  • Bleeding when brushing
  • Tender gums
  • Bad breath

What is Periodontitis?

Advanced gum disease. This happens when gingivitis is left untreated, potentially damaging the bone that supports your teeth.
