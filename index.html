Learn More About Oral Health

         

      Cavities are permanently damaged areas in the hard surface of your teeth that develop into tiny openings or holes caused by bacteria and tooth decay.     

         

      Gingivitis is a mild form of gum disease that causes irritation, redness, and swelling of your gums, often due to poor oral hygiene.     

         

      Periodontitis is a serious gum infection that damages soft tissue and can destroy the bone supporting your teeth if untreated.
